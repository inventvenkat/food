Butter Chicken (Murgh Makhani)

A rich and creamy North Indian chicken curry cooked in a tomato-based sauce with butter and cream.

Ingredients:
- 500g boneless chicken, cut into pieces  
- 4 large tomatoes, chopped
- 1/2 cup heavy cream
- 3 tbsp butter
- 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
- 1 tsp garam masala
- 1 tsp red chili powder
- 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
- 1 tsp sugar
- Salt to taste
- Fresh coriander for garnish
- 2 tbsp oil

For marinade:
- 1/2 cup yogurt
- 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
- 1/2 tsp red chili powder
- Salt

Method:
1. Marinate chicken with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, and salt for 30 minutes.
2. Heat oil in a pan and cook marinated chicken until 80% done. Set aside.
3. In the same pan, add butter and chopped tomatoes.
4. Cook tomatoes until they break down and form a thick sauce.
5. Add ginger-garlic paste, garam masala, red chili powder, turmeric, and sugar.
6. Add the cooked chicken pieces and mix well.
7. Pour in heavy cream and simmer for 8-10 minutes.
8. Adjust salt and spices to taste.
9. Garnish with fresh coriander.
10. Serve hot with naan or basmati rice.

Total time: 45 minutes
Servings: 4
