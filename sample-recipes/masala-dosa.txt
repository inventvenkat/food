Masala Dosa Recipe

A crispy South Indian crepe made from fermented rice and lentil batter, filled with spiced potato curry.

Ingredients:
- 2 cups dosa batter (fermented)
- 4 medium potatoes, boiled and cubed
- 1 large onion, sliced
- 2 green chilies, slit lengthwise
- 1 tsp mustard seeds
- 1 tsp cumin seeds
- 10-12 curry leaves
- 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
- Salt to taste
- 2 tbsp coconut oil
- Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions:
1. Heat oil in a pan, add mustard seeds and cumin seeds. When they splutter, add curry leaves and green chilies.
2. Add sliced onions and saut√© until translucent.
3. Add turmeric powder and boiled potatoes. Mix well and cook for 5 minutes.
4. Season with salt and garnish with coriander. Set aside the potato filling.
5. Heat a non-stick pan or dosa tawa over medium heat.
6. Pour a ladle of batter in the center and spread it thin in a circular motion.
7. Drizzle oil around the edges and cook until the bottom is golden and crispy.
8. Place the potato filling in the center of the dosa.
9. Fold the dosa over the filling and serve hot.
10. Serve with coconut chutney and sambar.

Prep time: 15 minutes
Cook time: 30 minutes
Serves: 4 people
