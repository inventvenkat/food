Classic Masala Dosa

Crispy rice crepe with spiced potato filling

Ingredients:
- dosa batter
- potatoes
- onions
- mustard seeds
- curry leaves
- turmeric
- green chilies
- Salt to taste
- Oil/butter as needed

Instructions:
1. Heat a non-stick pan or dosa tawa over medium heat.
2. Pour a ladle of batter in the center and spread thin.
3. Drizzle oil around the edges and cook until golden.
4. Add filling if making masala dosa.
5. Fold and serve hot with chutney and sambar.

Cooking time: 30 minutes
Serves: 4 people
